# Frequently Asked Questions

## Product Basics

**Is Reflex an exchange?**
No. Reflex is an execution terminal on top of Hyperliquid.

**Does Reflex custody funds?**
No. Funds remain in Hyperliquid account balances.

**Is there a subscription fee?**
No Reflex is free to access with traders only paying a 0.01% builder fee on trades executed.

## Access & Login

**Do I need waitlist/invite access?**
Yes, Reflex currently uses access gating.

**How do I log in?**
Use wallet connect + SIWE signature in the terminal.

## Trading

**What order types are available?**
Market, Limit, Scale, TWAP, and perp trigger variants.

**How many presets can I save?**
Up to 10.

**Can I trade from feed cards?**
Yes. Feed cards support Long/Short actions with active preset logic.

## Feed & Intelligence

**How many sources can I subscribe to?**
Up to 5 X accounts and 5 Telegram channels per user.

**Does Reflex include curated news by default?**
Yes, curated RSS/news feed sources are included.

**What does AI enrichment do?**
It adds sentiment/ticker context and can generate trade-idea suggestions.

## Notifications

**Which channels are stable today?**
Push notifications and Telegram are the main production channels for feed alerts.

## Telegram Bot

**What are the main commands?**
`/start`, `/trade`, `/wallet`, `/status`.

**Are `/long` and `/short` default commands?**
No, the current bot flow is command + guided conversation.

## Roadmap Surfaces

**Why do Stats/Points/Leaderboard look incomplete?**
Those sections are visible in navigation but still in staged rollout.
