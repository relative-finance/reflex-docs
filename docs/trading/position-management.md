# Position Management

Manage live positions from the `Positions` tab in Data Tables.

## Common Actions

- Market close
- Limit close
- Reverse position
- Add/edit TP/SL
- Adjust leverage
- Share position card

## Related Data Tabs

- `Open Orders` for resting orders
- `Funding History` for funding payments
- `Trade History` for fills

## Risk Control Tips

- Keep TP/SL coverage on leveraged positions
- Recheck liquidation distance after leverage changes
- Use reduce-only where appropriate when de-risking
