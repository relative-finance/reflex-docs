# Leverage & Margin

## Leverage

Leverage is configured per trade/preset.

- Asset-specific maximums apply
- Major markets can support up to 50x
- Reflex caps preset validation to the supported range

Higher leverage reduces your liquidation buffer.

## Margin Modes

Reflex supports:
- Cross
- Isolated

You can set margin mode in presets and adjust leverage on open positions.

## Liquidation Basics

Liquidation risk depends on:
- Entry
- Position size
- Leverage
- Margin mode
- Mark price movement

Use TP/SL and sensible sizing to control downside in volatile conditions.
