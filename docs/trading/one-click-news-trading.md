# One-Click News Trading

One-click trading lets you execute directly from feed cards.

## Flow

1. Select/confirm ticker on a feed item
2. Click `LONG` or `SHORT`
3. Reflex applies your active preset
4. Order is sent to Hyperliquid

## Confirmation Behavior

If your preset enables confirmation, Reflex opens a confirmation modal before submission.

## Quick Trade Sizes (Pulse)

In Pulse mode, each feed card includes quick size buttons for both long and short sides. These use the preset's quick-size values and bypass the standard confirmation flow for speed.

## Scope

Feed one-click trading is perp-focused in the current implementation.
