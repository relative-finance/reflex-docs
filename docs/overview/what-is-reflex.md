# What is Reflex?

Reflex is a trading terminal built on top of Hyperliquid.

It combines:
- Trading execution (spot + perps)
- Real-time feeds (X, Telegram, curated RSS/news)
- AI-enriched signal context
- Fast trade actions directly from feed items

## What Reflex Is Not

Reflex is not an exchange and does not custody funds.

- Orders execute on Hyperliquid
- Funds remain in your Hyperliquid account
- Settlement and matching are handled by Hyperliquid

## Main Product Surfaces

- Web terminal: `app.reflex.trade`
- Telegram bot workflow (linkable from terminal)

## Who It Is For

Reflex is designed for traders who want to move from signal to execution quickly, especially around news, events, and fast-moving market narratives.
