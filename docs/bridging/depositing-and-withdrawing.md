# Depositing & Withdrawing

## Depositing

You can fund Hyperliquid for Reflex trading by:

1. Bridging in-app (header `Bridge`)
2. Depositing directly through Hyperliquid

## Withdrawing

Withdrawals are handled on Hyperliquid, not directly from Reflex UI.

## In-App Balance Actions

From `Data Tables -> Balances`:

- `Transfer` moves USDC between Spot and Perps balances
- `Send` transfers tokens/USDC to another Hyperliquid address context

These are account actions on the Hyperliquid side, not custody transfers by Reflex.
