# News & Intelligence Overview

Reflex brings signal discovery and execution into one workflow.

## Sources

- X subscriptions
- Telegram channel subscriptions
- Curated RSS/news feed (default)

## AI Layer in Feed

Feed items can include:
- Sentiment label
- Refined ticker extraction

These are intended as decision support, not trade advice.

## Execution Integration

From each feed card you can:
- Select ticker(s)
- Execute Long/Short with active preset
- Request a generated trade idea (`Suggest Trade Idea`)

## Pulse Priority Behavior

In Pulse mode, feed ordering will prioritize items related to your currently open positions.
