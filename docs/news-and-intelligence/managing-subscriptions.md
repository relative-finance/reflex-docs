# Managing Subscriptions

Subscription management is available in onboarding and Settings.

## Limits

| Type | Limit |
|---|---|
| X accounts | 5 |
| Telegram channels | 5 |

## Add / Remove

1. Open `Settings -> Socials and RSS Feed`
2. Add handles/channels
3. Remove any source you no longer want

## Reset

The source management UI includes a reset option to clear your saved non-default subscriptions.

## Important

Curated/default feed sources are managed separately from your personal subscriptions.
