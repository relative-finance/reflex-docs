# Feed Sources

## X (Twitter)

You can subscribe to up to 5 X accounts per user.

## Telegram Channels

You can subscribe to up to 5 Telegram channels per user.

Only public/accessible channels are supported.

## Curated RSS / News Feed

Reflex includes a default curated feed stream for market-relevant headlines.

## Source Filters in UI

Feed filters include:
- All
- Telegram
- Twitter
- RSS Feed

These filters control what is displayed in the feed panel without deleting your saved subscriptions.
