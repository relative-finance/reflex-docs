# Connecting Your Wallet

Reflex uses Sign-In with Ethereum (SIWE).

## First-Time Access

1. Open `https://app.reflex.trade/trade`
2. Click `Connect`
3. If prompted, enter your invite code
4. Connect your wallet and sign the SIWE message

## Returning Users

If your wallet already has access, use `Already have access? Sign In` in the wallet popover and complete SIWE.

## What You Sign

SIWE signatures are authentication messages, not fund-transfer approvals.

## Security Notes

- Never share your seed phrase
- Verify the domain before signing
- Disconnect immediately if you suspect session compromise
