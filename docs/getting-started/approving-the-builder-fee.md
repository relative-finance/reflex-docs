# Approving the Builder Fee

Reflex routes orders through a Hyperliquid builder code.

## What You Approve

You sign a one-time approval that allows Reflex's builder address to receive builder fees on routed trades.

- Builder address: `0x3b8F84564e87b65B2d4B5686A40Ad9B496155cB8`
- Current web terminal builder fee target: `0.01%`

## When This Happens

During onboarding and/or your first eligible trade, Reflex checks your current approval and prompts if needed.

## Revoking Approval

You can revoke builder approval from Hyperliquid settings at any time.

## Notes

- This is separate from SIWE login
- It does not give Reflex custody of your funds
- Hyperliquid base trading fees still apply
